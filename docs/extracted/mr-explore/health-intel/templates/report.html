Disclaimer & Methodology

Data Sources: This report was compiled from publicly available data sources including the CMS Provider Data Catalog (data.cms.gov), IRS Form 990 filings via the ProPublica Nonprofit Explorer, the NPPES National Provider Identifier Registry, and ClinicalTrials.gov. All data is public record.

Accuracy: While every effort has been made to ensure accuracy, the data presented reflects the most recent available filings and may not represent the current state of the organization. Financial data is derived from IRS Form 990 filings which may be 12-18 months behind current operations. CMS quality data is updated periodically and may reflect measurement periods from prior years.

Not Financial Advice: This report is provided for informational and research purposes only. It does not constitute financial, investment, medical, or legal advice. Readers should conduct their own due diligence before making decisions based on this information.

Fair Use: All data used in this report is publicly available through U.S. government sources and nonprofit transparency requirements. No proprietary or confidential data was used in the preparation of this report.
